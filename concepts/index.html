Compound Calculator — 3 concepts

Three directions pulled from the working session + the doc notes. Best viewed on a phone (mobile-first, ~80% of the traffic). Each is a live, self-contained prototype — drag the sliders.

1

Live Duel

Instant, controllable, no reading

Radically simplified. The whole answer fits the first screen; a two-line chart (your strategy vs. the bank) redraws as you drag. Text-free sliders, floating allocation slider, a “why this number” drawer.

  • Notes #1 confusing → simplest possible
  • #3 comparison on screen 1, slider without text
  • #4 two lines: strategy vs bank saver
 2

Pick Your Character

Identify with a persona

The “человечки” idea. Instead of six static cards, a scroll row of characters — Grandma Saver, Copy Trader, Robo, the Investor, the Briefcase Trader, the Rolex Trader — feeding one adaptive card that becomes their money story.

  • Personas instead of abstract type-cards
  • One adaptive card (the end-of-meeting idea)
  • Mini bar-duel vs the bank
 3

Do Nothing

Organised by time-cost

Leads with note #6 — the hands-off tier (Robo · Copy · Investor). One “how much time can you give it?” slider drives everything, plus the ✓/✗ comparison table from the sketch.

  • #6 “graph where nothing needs doing”
  • Effort slider as the main control
  • ✓/✗ comparison grid from the notes

Current production version stays at the repo root index.html. These three don’t replace it — they’re concepts to react to.
